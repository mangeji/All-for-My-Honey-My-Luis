For You

This page contains my love for you. Sometimes, I find it hard to look for words to express my love for you, so instead of just telling you "I love you" everytime, I wrote my feelings down.

I’m pretty sure it wasn’t when I told him “I love you.”

It wasn’t when he became my partner nor when I started being shy around him. It was when he became my life. Not because I can’t leave without him nor because he’s my life support, but rather, when he became a reason to live. I knew I loved him the moment he made the thought of living better than death. He was like a lantern who found me lost on my path and lit my way back home.

I knew I loved him when I didn’t have to catch up with his humor so I can laugh at his jokes. I didn’t have to pretend. I didn’t have to hide my colors. Laughing with him came naturally and I then realized sooner that even his corniest punchline can make me giggle.

I knew I loved him when he hugged my shadow and caressed it gently instead of running away. He never made me feel I was unworthy of love. He patiently waited on my front door till I was ready to open it — and when I did, he waited once again until I was willing to welcome him in my arms.

I knew I loved him when I woke up in his arms. Instead of being worried if he finds me too clingy as I lay on his chest, I found peace in him. The once chaotic mind I have suddenly went silent as I slowly drifted into sleep. His heartbeat became a lullaby and I never thought he would be the most comfortable pillow I can have.

I knew I loved him when I saw him look at me with stars in his eyes. They were shining brightly staring at me like I was the only one that matters at that moment. He told me I was pretty. He told me I was beautiful… and every time he said those words, I felt like a baby who heard it the first time.

I knew I loved him when I suddenly grabbed my pen and started writing prose for him. It has already been so long since I stopped writing, yet as I think of him, the words just kept flowing through my mind like a river — never ending like an ocean. I never realized I was that romantic until I read what I wrote. I saw half of my heart in it and I saw how happy I am because of him.

I knew I loved him even before he said he loves me. It wasn’t an abrupt realization. I didn’t suddenly wake up realizing it. It was gradual and it kept growing. I didn’t fall for him. It never felt like that. Loving him is like bathing under the rain. I just knew I’d love it. I knew it would be worth it. I knew I’d be happy accepting him in my life with arms wide open.

Or maybe he does.

I sometimes find myself thinking how lucky I got to have him in my life. When was the last time I felt so safe? When was the last time I felt this confident? When was the last time I was this happy? All those buried feelings were brought back when he broke down my walls and showed me his soul.

With him, I found comfort even in silence. I can be dumb without judgement. With him, I feel loved. He makes me laugh. He makes me smile. He makes me happy…

Yet, sometimes he makes me cry in the middle of the night.

He treats me so well. He showed me love that I never knew I deserved to receive. He makes my heart squeal in joy and the next thing I know, I am already crying. Crying my eyes out because I can’t contain how much I love him, how much I appreciate him, and how happy I am with him.

He never made me cry in pain, however, he does make me cry out of happiness. I am such a crybaby for my man that I cry when I remember how soft and gentle he is with me. He takes care of me so well. He does things for me without being told. I cry when I remember how understanding and patient he is. Even on days I chose to isolate myself, he’s always there waiting and understanding every reason I have. He may not be the best at comforting, but he is a great listener.

He is the best boyfriend. My lover who always does his best for me. The man who spends his last money to spend time with me. My darling who told me he’ll fight for me and stay even if the only thing that is clinging to me is his last strand of hair. My baby, my love, mi cariño.

But at what cost?

No one can love an angry complicated woman.

But he did.

No man likes a hyper independent woman.

But he did.

No one can be patient enough to stay with a woman who has an unhealed heart.

But he did.

The things I never once thought I’m deserving of, he proved me wrong. He changed my perception. He gave me a new scenery of love to look over. He gave me a reason to give love another chance. He changed me.

He understood that I was used to doing things alone and yet offered me his hand. He understood that I value my alone time and yet he offered me his presence. He knew that behind all this facade of independency is just a girl who wants to be held; A girl who wants to be told that she did a great job on holding on during tough times. A girl who’s been through a lot. A girl whose anger is a symbol of loneliness. He knew that it would be a complicated maze. He knew that risking might even hurt him but even so, he chose to hug the thorns. He changed me at the cost of being better.

I never knew I needed someone to open the door for me until he did. I never knew my thoughts matter until he validated my every emotion. I never knew that my anger could turn into a sweet ray of sunlight. I never knew that love could feel so magical. Day by day, I find myself loving him more than I did yesterday. Day by day, I find myself thinking of how much more can I love this man for the next days.

He made me want to change myself. The self-sabotages and negative talks of “he doesn’t deserve me” turned into “I want to keep his smile.” I knew I wasn’t worthy of his love and so, I decided to be worthy of it because I know no one can love me more than he did. No one can embrace me in a hug warmer than his. No one can go through such lengths for me unless him, and no one can be my darling if it’s not him.
